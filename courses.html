Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Undergraduate Programs

Daffodil International University offers diverse undergraduate programs across various fields, including Computer Science, Business, Engineering, and Arts. These programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in the global workforce.

Post Graduation

DIU also provides a range of postgraduate courses focusing on advanced education and research in disciplines such as Technology, Business Administration, and Engineering. These programs emphasize innovation and leadership in specialized fields.

Library

DIU has 60000 square feet of library space at Daffodil Smart City, with beautiful collections of books, journals, magazines, reference books, and research papers, both online and hardcopy, available at DIU.

Transport

DIU has an extensive transport pool used by students, faculty members, and officials. Students, faculty members, and employees can move from distant areas to their respective campuses through this transport facility. Students can use transport facilities for industry tours, study tours, annual picnics etc.

Gymnasium and Swimming Pool

DIU provides top-notch facilities for students' well-being, including a gym, playground, and swimming pool. These amenities support both physical and mental growth, and the university stresses proper usage and maintenance by everyone in the DIU community.

One Student One Laptop

We live in a world of information technology where education is increasingly dependent on computers and the internet. It has now been the prime responsibility of the university to equip its students with proper technical knowledge so they can survive and secure a respectable place in society.

Laboratory Facilities

DIU has embraced digitalization with high-speed internet, Wi-Fi, free laptops, and digital media tools to foster innovative learning. This infrastructure aligns with Vision 2021 and Digital Bangladesh, preparing students for the modern workforce.

Accommodation

DIU has arranged a spacious, secured and nearby residence for male & female students, where students reside in comfort, intensive care and in the lap of a study environment. The serene atmosphere of these accommodations is provided to the requirements of the students.
